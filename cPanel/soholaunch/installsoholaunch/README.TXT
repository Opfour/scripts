1) Upload installsoholaunch.php to your sites docroot folder (usualy named public_html, www, httpdocs, or something similar).

2) Open up your web browser (internet explorer, or firefox) and go to http://yourdomainname.com/installsoholaunch.php and follow the instructions.

3) For information on other installation methods, and licensing, visit http://soholaunch.com .

System Requirements:
1. IBM compatible PC with Windows 98 or better, or Mac with Virtual PC (or another program similar to Virtual PC). Will not work from Linux desktop.
2. 64Mb of Ram or better.
3. Supported Browsers: Internet Explorer v5.2+, FireFox v1.07+, Netscape v8+, Flock 0.5+
4. 800 x 600 Screen resolution or better.
